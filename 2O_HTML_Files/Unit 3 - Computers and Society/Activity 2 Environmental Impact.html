Computers and Society

In class we will learn about the many impacts that computers, mobile devices & the Internet have had on our society, our environment, our ethics & our careers, you will have an opportunity to demonstrate what you have learned.

Evidence of Learning

Impact on the Environment - Advertisement

For this task, you will choose a local (Ontario) company that offers computer and electronic recycling services. Imagine that they want a new advertising campaign that highlights their environmentally-friendly and socially responsible recycling practices. You will create an advertisement for them, using ONE of the following formats:

Video (youtube or TV ad) - Use a tool like WeVideo (link on the student portal) or Screencastify

Print (poster or billboard) - Using Google Slides

Your ad must include the following information:

  • The name of the company and the company logo
  • A description of why recyling electronics is so important (include relevant facts or statistics)
  • What electronics that the company will accept
  • Reasons why someone should choose this company for their recyling needs, including any certifications they have obtained to validate their practices
  • Where to find more information (website address)
