Computers and Society

In class we will learn about the many impacts that computers, mobile devices & the Internet have had on our society, our environment, our ethics & our careers, you will have an opportunity to demonstrate what you have learned.

Evidence of Learning

Ethical Issues - Demonstration of Understanding

For this task, choose one of the ethical or legal issues pertaining to the use of technology discussed in class, and demonstrate your understanding of this issue & its consequences using one of the following formats:

Advice column - Create a fictional situation that features an ethical or legal dilemma, then write a response that highlights both the short and long term consequences that should be considered.

Mind Map - Create a static or dynamic mind map using a tool like MindMup, Explain Everything (link on the student portal), or another tool of your choosing to demonstrate your understanding of the issue chosen. Again, your mind map should include both the short and long term consequences that should be considered.
